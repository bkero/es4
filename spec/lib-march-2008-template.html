ECMAScript 4th Edition
[proposed]

Predefined Types and Objects


Incomplete draft - 21 March 2008




Abstract

This document presents draft specifications for some of the pre-defined types and objects in proposed ECMAScript 4. Eventually the document will incorporate approved specifications for all the pre-defined types and objects, and it will itself be incorporated into the final language standard.

All the drafts that are presented in this document have been subject to at least one round of discussion and refinement on the ''es4-discuss@mozilla.org'' mailing list; most drafts have been through several rounds and are now considered more or less complete.

All of the drafts included in this document will be subject to (primarily minor) revisions as the language specification is developed over the next half year. In cases where the revisions are non-trivial or non-obvious they will be discussed on the ''es4-discuss'' list.

This document includes a short tutorial introduction to ES4 that provides the reader with some of the necessary background for understanding the draft specifications. A high degree of familiarity with ES3 is additionally required.

= Types and objects not yet defined =

The following types and objects slated for inclusion in the specification have not yet been discussed on the es4-discuss list, or the discussion has not progressed far enough to warrant inclusion in the present draft:

  • ''Array''
  • ''String'' and ''string''
  • ''Boolean'' and ''boolean''
  • ''Number'' and ''double''
  • ''Math''
  • ''Date''
  • ''RegExp''
  • ''Vector''
  • Reflection interfaces
